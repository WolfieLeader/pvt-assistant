# Product Positioning — Audience & Competitors

## Target Audience

- Young and busy Israelis, 16-30 — students, early career
- Want to take finances seriously but hate spreadsheets and complex apps
- Israeli credit culture: installments are universal — interest-free and with interest
- Multi-currency reality: NIS primary but $ for online shopping, EUR for travel
- Privacy-conscious generation — skeptical about sharing bank credentials

## Pvt. Unique Value Proposition

1. **Privacy-first** — all data on-device, no cloud sync, no bank connections. Your financial data never leaves your phone.
2. **Installment tracking (split payments)** — THE Israeli feature. Interest-free and with-interest installments, parent/child expense relationships, "3/12" badge on feed. No competitor does this well.
3. **On-device AI insights** — personalized spending tips powered by on-device LLM. No data sent to servers. 1-2 insights per day.
4. **Feed UX** — Instagram-like scrollable feed, not a spreadsheet. Opens fast, logs fast. Designed for the generation that grew up on social feeds.
5. **Work-hours equivalent** — "that dinner cost you 3 hours of work" — makes spending tangible and personal.
6. **Return window tracking** — track when you can still return purchases. Smart nudge 3 days before window closes. Israeli consumer pattern nobody else addresses.
7. **Income tracking** — full financial picture: net cash flow, savings rate, spend-to-earn ratio.
8. **Multi-currency** — NIS + USD + EUR display throughout. Essential for Israeli lifestyle (online shopping in USD, travel in EUR).
